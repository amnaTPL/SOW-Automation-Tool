Exhibit Preview — Details & Definitions

A. Forensic Service Case Parameters

  • All forensic collections and analysis services are provided by qualified forensic practitioners using industry-standard tools and methods.
  • Standard acquisitions include logical and physical device acquisitions, cloud collections, and email archive collections.
  • All collections are documented with appropriate chain-of-custody records.
  • Forensic consulting includes project oversight, client consultation, and review of forensic methodology.
  • Senior forensic consulting is required for remediation work, expert testimony, affidavit preparation, and advanced analytics.
  • After-hours and weekend rates apply to services scheduled outside of standard business hours (09:00–18:00 Mon–Fri, excluding UK public holidays).

B. eDiscovery Definitions

  • Native Review: Review of documents in their original file format.
  • ECA (Early Case Assessment): Evaluating a collection before full review to understand scope and relevance.
  • Processing: Converting raw data into reviewable format, including de-duplication, deNIST filtering, and indexing.
  • Hosting: Providing a secure web-based review environment (Relativity or similar).
  • TAR (Technology Assisted Review): Machine learning algorithms assisting in identifying relevant documents.
  • Brainspace: Conceptual analytics and TAR platform integrated with Relativity.
  • Productions: Disclosure of relevant documents to opposing parties in agreed format.
  • Data Disposition: Secure deletion or return of client data at matter conclusion.

C. Managed Review

  • All managed reviewers are legally qualified or have equivalent experience and are trained on TransPerfect Legal's quality management protocols.
  • Review Managers are responsible for day-to-day review management, issue escalation, and quality control.
  • Project Managers provide strategic oversight, coordinating with counsel and ensuring timelines are met.
  • All review is conducted in accordance with an agreed review protocol, prepared in consultation with the instructing law firm.
  • TransPerfect Legal maintains a continuous quality assurance programme with a minimum 10% QC rate applied to all reviewer decisions.
  • Daily reporting on review progress is provided as standard.

D. Case Closure Options

  • At matter conclusion, TransPerfect Legal will work with Client to agree appropriate data disposition procedures.
  • Options include: secure deletion; return of data on encrypted media; transfer to a third-party platform.
  • All data disposition is documented and a certificate of destruction/transfer provided upon request.
  • Archive storage is available at reduced rates for concluded matters where data must be retained for a defined period.
  • Database disposal fees apply for deletion of Relativity databases (flat fee under 1TB; per-GB over 1TB).
